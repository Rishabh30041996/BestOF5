Best Five PC Racing Games

5: The Crew

Genre :Racing

Publisher : Ubisoft

Platforms : Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox 360, Xbox One

First release : December 2, 2014(The Crew)

Latest release: 29 Jun, 2018(The Crew 2)

About Game : The newest iteration in the revolutionary franchise, The Crew® 2 captures the thrill of the American motorsports spirit in one of the most exhilarating open worlds ever created. Welcome to Motornation, a huge, varied, action-packed, and beautiful playground built for motorsports throughout the entire US of A. Enjoy unrestrained exploration on ground, sea, and sky.

Minimum System Requirements:

OS :Windows 7 SP1, Windows 8.1, Windows 10 (64-bit versions only)

Processor : Intel Core i5-2400s @ 2.5 GHz or AMD FX-6100 @ 3.3 GHz or equivalent

Memory : 8 GB RAM

Video Card : NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD HD 7870

Sound Card: 100% DirectX 10 compatible

HDD Space : 30GB

4: Dirt Rally

Genre :Racing

Publisher : Codemasters

Platforms : Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, macOS, Linux

First release : 7 December 2015(Dirt Rally)

Latest release : 26 Feb, 2019(Dirt Rally 2.0)

About Game : DiRT Rally 2.0 dares you to carve your way through a selection of iconic rally locations from across the globe, in the most powerful off-road vehicles ever made, knowing that the smallest mistake could end your stage. You will need to rely on your instincts with the most immersive and truly focused off-road experience yet, including a new authentic handling model, tyre choice and surface degradation.

Minimum System Requirements:

OS : 64bit versions of Windows® 7, Windows® 8, Windows® 10

Processor : AMD FX4300 or Intel Core i3 2130

Memory : 8 GB RAM

Video Card : AMD HD7750 or NVIDIA GTX650Ti

Sound Card: DirectX® Compatible soundcard

HDD Space : 100 GB available space

DirectX: Version 11

Network : Broadband Internet connection

3: Forza

Genre :Racing

Publisher : Turn 10 Studios, Playground Games, Sumo Digital

Platforms : Xbox, Xbox 360, Xbox One, Xbox Series X and Series S, Microsoft Windows, iOS, Android

Latest release : April 15, 2019(Forza Street)

2: F1® 2020

Genre :Racing

Publisher : Codemasters

Platforms : Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Stadia
