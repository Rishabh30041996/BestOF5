Best Five PC Adventure Games

5: Fallout

Genre :Open WorldAdventureRPG

Publisher : Bethesda Softworks

Platforms : MacOS, Microsoft Windows, Xbox, Playsation, Android, iOS

First release : October 10, 1997(Fallout)

Latest release: November 14, 2018(Fallout 76)

About Game : Steel Dawn is the first chapter in the new Brotherhood of Steel questline, free for Fallout 76 players. Paladin Leila Rahmani and her troops have arrived from California to establish a new Appalachian chapter. Work with or against the other Factions to achieve success. Visit settlements populated with new NPCs and unlock powerful weapons and armor from the Brotherhood arsenal.

Minimum System Requirements:

OS :Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit versions)

Processor : Intel Core i5-6600k 3.5 GHz /AMD Ryzen 3 1300X 3.5 GHz or equivalent

Memory : 8 GB RAM

Video Card : NVIDIA GTX 780 3GB /AMD Radeon R9 285 2GB or equivalent

Network: Broadband Internet connection

HDD Space : 80 GB available space

4: Cyberpunk 2077

Genre :Open WorldAdventureRPGSci-Fi

Publisher : CD PROJEKT RED

Platforms : PlayStation 4, Google Stadia, Xbox One, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X and Series S, Microsoft Windows

First release : 10 Dec, 2020

About Game : Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure story set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour and body modification. You play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality. You can customize your character’s cyberware, skillset and playstyle, and explore a vast city where the choices you make shape the story and the world around you.

Minimum System Requirements:

OS : Windows 7 or 10, 64-bit processor and operating system

Processor : Intel Core i5-3570K or AMD FX-8310

Memory : 8 GB RAM

Video Card : NVIDIA GeForce GTX 780 or AMD Radeon RX 470

DirectX: Version 12

HDD Space : 70 GB available space

Genre :ActionAdventureCasual

Publisher : Telltale

Platforms : Android, iOS, OS X, PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, PlayStation Vita, Windows, Xbox 360, Xbox One
