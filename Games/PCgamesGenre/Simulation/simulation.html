Best Five PC Simulation Games

5: Assetto Corsa

Genre :RacingSimulationSports

Publisher : KUNOS Simulazioni

Platforms : Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One

First release : December 19, 2014(Assetto Corsa)

Latest release: 18 Nov, 2020(Assetto Corsa Competizione - 2020 GT World Challenges)

About Game : Assetto Corsa is a racing simulation that attempts to offer a realistic driving experience with a variety of road and race cars through detailed physics and tyre simulation on race tracks recreated through laser-scanning technology.

Minimum System Requirements:

OS :Windows 7 64-bit Service Pack 1

Processor : Intel Core i5-4460 or AMD FX-8120

Memory : 4 GB RAM

Video Card : GeForce GTX 460 2GB, Radeon HD 7770

DirectX: Version 11

HDD Space : 50 GB available space

4: ARMA

Genre : Military simulation Tactical shooter

Publisher : Bohemia Interactive

Platforms : Microsoft Windows, Xbox, Linux, Android, iOS

First release : November 10, 2006(ARMA: Armed Assault)

First release : September 12, 2013(ARMA 3)

About Game : Experience true combat gameplay in a massive military sandbox. Deploying a wide variety of single- and multiplayer content, over 20 vehicles and 40 weapons, and limitless opportunities for content creation, this is the PC’s premier military game. Authentic, diverse, open - Arma 3 sends you to war.

Minimum System Requirements:

OS :Windows 7 SP1 (64bit)

Processor : Intel Dual-Core 2.4 GHz or AMD Dual-Core Athlon 2.5 GHz

Memory : 4 GB RAM

Video Card : NVIDIA GeForce 9800GT / AMD Radeon HD 5670 / Intel HD Graphics 4000 with 512 MB VRAM

Sound Card: DirectX®-compatible on-board

HDD Space : 45 GB free space

Genre :combat flight simulatorAction

Publisher : Gaijin Distribution KFT

Platforms : Microsoft Windows, MacOS, Linux, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S

First release : December 21, 2016

Join now and take part in major battles on land, in the air, and at sea, fighting with millions of players from all over the world in an ever-evolving environment.
